My Start

I started my jiu jitsu journey about six years ago as a white belt. I was doing a lot of Muay Thai and watching a lot of Mixed Martial Arts and so it started off as something to make me a more well rounded martial artist and mostly something to get me in shape. Who knew it would turn into a life's obsession. I consider it a part of my lifestyle as it has blended into many parts of my life and self. I have gotten healthier and stronger, gained many friendships, and have had a found a creative outlet as result of doing jiu jitsu. Hopefully I could convince you to give it a try. I really believe jiu jitsu is for everyone.

Benefits of Jiu Jitsu

  1. Teaches value of working hard - You will quickly learn that hard work beats talent when talent refuses to work hard and so you adapt. This typically starts to align with rest of your life.
  2. Makes you face your fears - it is scary to get hurt but once you face that you can face anything
  3. You get strong - the harder you work the stronger you get
  4. The mentality gets stronger - you learn manage fear, fight through discomfort and teaches you how to fight back.
